Weddings

Your Unforgettable Day, One Unforgettable Place

The Milano Park Hotel offers a one-of-a-kind setting for an intimate, romantic Italy Wedding. With a location in one of most highly coveted neighborhoods, comprehensive Wedding Planning Services and a myriad of elegant Event Spaces, Milano Park Hotel is the place for a Italy Wedding. From the Bridal Shower to the Ceremony to the Post-Wedding Brunch, the Hotel specializes in creating truly custom celebrations. Each Wedding includes a dedicated professional Special Events team ready to simplify, organize and customize every last detail, like recommending the most discerning floral, invitation and wedding cake designers.

OTHER HOTEL AMENITIES INCLUDE:

  • Bamboo Chairs for the Ceremony and Dinner
  • Long tables with White Linens and Napkins
  • Abundant Candlelight
  • State-of-the-art DJ Equipment
  • Wireless Microphones for Speeches
  • Personalized Menus

While there are multiple picturesque Event Spaces to choose from, none is more sought after for Weddings than the 18th floor Gramercy Terrace. The Terrace is composed of interior salons filled with 20th century art and lush exterior gardens that wrap around the rooftop. Its fully weatherized retractable glass enclosure provides unparalleled views of Manhattan and allows for beautiful New York Weddings all year round. This stunning space can accommodate up to 120 guests for a seated dinner with dancing or up to 160 guests for a cocktail-style reception. Ceremony space is available with varying formats for up to 120 guests.

The Milano Park Hotel’s sophisticated reception areas are matched with exceptional menu choices. Danny Meyer’s Union Square Hospitality Group has created an extensive selection of masterful dishes to choose from. Whether enjoying a relaxed buffet-style celebration in a lounge setting or an elegant 3-course Dinner for a sophisticated seated reception, the menu may be tailored to individual tastes and it can be enhanced by a number of supplemental dishes and Chef stations, like a Sushi Bar, Carving Station or Gelato Table.
