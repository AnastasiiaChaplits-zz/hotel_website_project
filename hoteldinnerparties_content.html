Dinner Parties

A Delightful Epicurean Experience

The Milano Park Hotel is an elegant Milano Hotel that specializes in custom Dinner Parties for Birthdays, Anniversaries and other celebratory occasions. Enjoy a relaxed buffet style Event, choosing amongst a variety of passed hors d’oeuvres, reception stations and specialty cocktails. For a seated dinner reception, create a custom 3-course menu with dishes ranging from Tuna or Steak Tartare to Pan Roasted Rack of Lamb with Fingerling Potatoes to Double Chocolate Mousse Cake.
The Hotel’s professional Events Team is on hand to help choose the perfect menu, and execute the dinner flawlessly. Dinner Parties can be enjoyed in any of the Hotel’s multiple premiere Event Spaces — including the glamorous Milano Terrace, where Guests can enjoy sweeping views of Manhattan and works by Andy Warhol and Damien Hirst.
